I am an AI Researcher at [Illuin Technology](https://www.illuin.tech/) (Paris), working on NLP and multimodal AI for industrial applications. My current work spans visual document retrieval, speech processing, and the development of production-grade AI systems.

I hold a Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence from the [University of Surrey](https://www.surrey.ac.uk/) (2023–2024), where I was awarded the International Excellence Scholarship. My master's thesis — *Zero-shot and Few-shot Adaptation of Multimodal Foundation Models* — was supervised by Dr Muhammad Awais Rana. I also hold a General Engineering degree from [École Centrale de Lyon](https://www.ec-lyon.fr/) (2021–2024), with specializations in AI, machine learning, and applied mathematics.

## Research Interests

- Multimodal AI and vision-language models
- Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) and information retrieval
- Natural language processing and speech processing
- Computer vision and visual document understanding
